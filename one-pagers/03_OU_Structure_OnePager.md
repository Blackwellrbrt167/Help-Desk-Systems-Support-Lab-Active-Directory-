Organizational Unit Structure

# Objective
Design and implement a structured Organizational Unit (OU) hierarchy to reflect a real-world enterprise environment.

# Why This Matters
OUs provide administrative control, delegation, and policy targeting. A clean OU structure is essential for scalability, security, and operational efficiency.

# Actions Performed
Created top-level organizational OUs
Built department-based sub-OUs
Separated users, computers, and groups logically
Enabled protection against accidental deletion
Validated OU hierarchy consistency

# Validation
OU structure visible in Active Directory Users and Computers
No objects left in default containers
Accidental deletion protection confirmed
